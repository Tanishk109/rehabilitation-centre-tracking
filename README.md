# 🏥 Rehabilitation Centre Tracking System

A centralized full-stack platform designed to manage, monitor, and optimize operations across rehabilitation centres in India. The system enables efficient patient tracking, recovery monitoring, and inventory management through a role-based administrative structure.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The **Rehabilitation Centre Tracking System** streamlines workflows across multiple rehabilitation centres using a hierarchical system:

- **Super Admin (Central Authority)**
- **Node Admins (Centre-Level Authorities)**

The platform is scalable to manage:

- **495+ Rehabilitation Centres**
  - 340 IRCAs (Integrated Rehabilitation Centres for Addicts)
  - 71 ODICs (Outreach and Drop-In Centres)
  - 48 CPLIs
  - 21 SLCAs
  - 15 DDACs  
- **46 ATFs (Addiction Treatment Facilities)** across India

---

## 🧩 Features

### 🔐 Role-Based Access Control
- **Super Admin**
  - Manages all centres and admins
  - Access to global data and insights  

- **Node Admin**
  - Manages centre-specific operations
  - Updates patient and inventory data  

---

### 🧑‍⚕️ Patient Management
- Add, update, and manage patient records  
- Maintain treatment history and medical data  

---

### 📊 Recovery Tracking
- Track patient progress and recovery stages  
- Monitor treatment outcomes  

---

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Manage medicines and resources  
- Track stock levels across centres  

---

### 🌐 Scalability
- Supports 495+ centres efficiently  
- Modular architecture for expansion  

---
